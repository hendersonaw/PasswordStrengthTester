Password Strength Analyzer

This web page allows you to test the strength of your password and give you a score based on length and use of alphanumeric and non-alphanumeric characters.

Getting started

This tool will determine if your email address has been in a security breach, as well as perform a strength assessment on the given password. Simply type in your email address and a password in the provided fields to determine the strength of your account.

NOTE: Never, ever give out your username/password to an untrusted website!
