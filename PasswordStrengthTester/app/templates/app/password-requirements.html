NIST Password Recommendations for Ballad Health

When choosing a password, it is important to use the newest security recommendations to improve the quality and strength of your personal and professional accounts. The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) recommend the following password requirements:

  1. Regular user accounts should be 10 characters or longer. Administrative accounts should be 15 characters or longer.
  2. Inclusion of spaces increases the overall entropy of the password and is encouraged.
  3. NIST recommends the use of regular English words constructed into normal, understandable passphrases, rather than a complex string of text that is un-readable and hard to understand.
  4. While NIST has removed the requirement for regular forced password resets, Ballad's Compliance and Legal Department still require password resets every 180 days, and Ballad archives the last 10 passwords for each user.
  5. Do not use common passwords such as "password", "123456", or anything resembling them.
  6. Do not use words such as "BalladHealth", "Ballad Health", "Ballad2021", "Ballad2022", "BHealth2021", "BHealth2022", (other forms where the year may be used, along with any "special character(s)" at the end).
